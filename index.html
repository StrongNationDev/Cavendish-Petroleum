In 2003, Cavendish Petroleum Oil & Gas Company Limited was awarded 70% interest in Umusadege Field located in PML 22 (formerly OML 56) which is situated in the northern area of Delta State, Nigeria. Cavendish Petroleum is the operator of the field while SunTrust Atlantic Energies (formerly SunTrust Oil Company Nigeria Limited) its Joint Venture Partner holds a 30% interest which is jointly held by Mart Umusadege Resources Nigeria Limited (MURNL), the Risk Service Provider to the Joint Venture. In 2005, Cavendish Petroleum Oil & Gas Company Limited commenced its upstream activities.
