Our Culture

We understand that social stewardship and effective engagement with internal stakeholders and external stakeholders are critical to earning and sustaining our license to operate. Hence, we have consciously worked toward ensuring that we are a people-focused organization with a culture of treating our corporate and social responsibility with utmost priority.

Our Commitment

As a leading independent E & P Company with a diversified portfolio of hydrocarbon assets whose mission it is to explore and develop hydrocarbon in a safe, environmentally friendly and cost-efficient manner, we are strongly committed to sustainability. We are committed to exploring and producing hydrocarbon assets in a responsible manner in line with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 17.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to explore and develop hydrocarbon in a safe, environmentally friendly and cost-efficient manner.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to be a leading independent E & P Company, with a diversified portfolio of hydrocarbon assets.
